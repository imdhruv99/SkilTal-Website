Who we are

We the directors & strategists, enriching student's fundamental education.

Welcome to SkilTal come with us, we deliver learning with fun activities and real-life experiments. We are dedicated to bring out the best in every student by imparting a strong educational foundation. Given the dynamic and global nature of education in the 21st century, we are constantly working hard and reinventing ourselves with the ultimate goal of creating exceptional and enriching student experiences. Here at Skiltal, Students get a chance to level up their academics, dive deep into a subject of interest, and develop future-ready skills. We nurture the aspirations of junior class students for various competitive and scholarship examinations such as NTSE, Olympiads, etc. And prepare them for school/board examinations. We are consistently working towards providing students with the finest of learning possibilities with which they can excel and make a brighter career. We believe focusing on building a solid foundation of scientific and technical knowledge and thus preparing competent and motivated engineers and scientists. We aim to create an environment for freedom of thought, cultivate vision, encourage growth, develop personality and self-discipline for the pursuit of excellence.
